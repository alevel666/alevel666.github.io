Opportunity cost

200306-10-Q1

A student decides to stay in her room to do some work rather than going to the cinema.
What is the opportunity cost of her decision?

答案:

分析Analysis

Opportunity cost is the value of the best alternative forgone when making an economic choice.

机会成本是做一个经济决定时”放弃的最好的其他选择的价值”.

  • When deciding to “stay in her room”, she gave up the best alternative (benefits of) “going to the cinema”, thus A.

当决定留在家里做作业时, 放弃了去电影院娱乐得到的好处, 正确答案A.

  • “the improvement in the mark” is the benefits she will obtain, NOT benefits forgone.

“成绩提高”是她做完这个决定后得到的好处, 不是“因为这个决定放弃的其他好处”.

  • “the cost of extra electricity” is part of direct costs caused by the current choice, NOT best alternative benefits forgone

“多花的电费”是做这个决定的直接成本, 不是“放弃的其他选择中最好的”

  • “the money saved” is benefits due to current choice, NOT best alternative benefits forgone

“省下的钱”是现在决定的好处，不是“放弃的其他选择中最好的”
